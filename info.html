About Us

PM Infotech and Securities is a wholesaler in CCTV and peripherals in Hyderabad, a new-age Internet of Things (IoT) company with a vision of being the largest provider of Electronic Surveillance (E-Surveillance) systems in India. We offer customized solutions for intrusion detection, e-surveillance, and access control to meet the high-value asset protection needs of our clients.

Many gated community apartments, villas, townships, shopping malls, private and multinational companies, retail outlets, warehouses, schools, colleges, and jewellery stores use our solutions to monitor and protect their assets.

We provide CCTV installation services in Hyderabad. With state-of-the-art intruder alarm systems, over 1500+ sites are being protected and 2000+ sites are being monitored by our E-Surveillance System.

Our sophisticated research and design environment enables us to deliver comprehensive engineering solutions for new cutting-edge technologies in intrusion detection and prevention. We believe in not only developing and delivering products that meet customer needs but also ensuring our innovation processes are positioned for the future.
